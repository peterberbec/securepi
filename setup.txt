Preperation:

1. Write down your Wifi name (SSID) and password.
2. Download your OpenVPN client configuration from your VPN provider.
3. Write down your VPN username and password.
4. Connect SecurePi to your computer with the included USB cable.
5. Open a web browser and wait for the SecurePi screen to display.
6. Go to the website http://secure.pi and accept the security warning.
  This warning is because the SSL certificate used to encrypt traffic between your computer and
  the SecurePi is self-signed. This presents no security concerns with this setup.
7. Click Configuration and follow the instructions.
